Company Info

Established in 2001, Reservations Gateway is an end to end IT solutions and service company. Headquartered in Reston VA, we provide best in class and cost effective enterprise level software solutions, IT infrastructure management, IT consultancy, and Business Process Outsourcing services to organizations of all sizes.
Our outcome driven approach (on our customers’ desired outcomes) aligns our team to deliver world class solutions with flexibility and speed. Whether you’re looking to outsource all of your IT services or simply need to augment your existing IT staff, we have flexible business solutions that will meet your needs.
We take pride in the many tributes we’ve received from our customers who have grown their businesses profitably due to the solutions developed by us, and, we value our position as a true tech partner, with each one of our customers.

VISION

We help organizations harness the power of innovation, data & technology to deliver better business outcomes

MISSION STATEMENT

Provide businesses a one vendor solution covering end to end IT services to ease their challenges, and, by delivering well designed, best in class, cost effective solutions with greater flexibility and speed.

OUR VALUES

We are passionate about the success of our customers & our employees. We grow by creating successful customers and driving innovation in whatever we do. We make it exceptionally easy for our customers to do business with us. We conduct business with the highest ethical standard, & strive to build long-term client relationships based on mutual trust and respect. Our team of diverse, highly talented team members are aligned in the relentless pursuit of our common goal.

Mesfin Eyob

Mesfin is President/CEO and co-founder of Rezgateway. He has overall responsibility for the company's performance and strategic direction. An expert in management, finance, international business, process optimization, and quality control; he has worked for half a dozen companies in senior management positions.

Prior to founding Rezgateway, Mesfin was President/City Executive of Wachovia Bank (the fourth largest bank in the US) in Northern Virginia for 17 years; CFO and then President for Middle East and Africa of Apex CoVantage, the leader in high performance global Knowledge Process Outsourcing solutions. In addition, Mesfin is co-founder and chairman of a charitable NGO, Ascend Sustainable Foundation, engaged in AIDS prevention charity work in Ethiopia. Mesfin holds a BA from Washington DC University and an MBA from American University.

Ashok Senadeera

Ashok is the COO and co-founder of Rezgateway. An operations and technology executive with more than 22 years of experience in all facets of running technology related businesses. He has a deep understanding of E-commerce and the Travel ecosystem.

Ashok oversees the company’s day-to-day operations as well as providing leadership for sales, marketing, product development and technology. As an experienced leader, Ashok is always striving to find innovative solutions and is committed to continuous improvement in all facets of the business.

Prior to forming Rezgateway, Ashok held various leadership positions in product design, project management, sales, marketing and business development in finance, travel and technology companies. Ashok holds a Bachelor of Commerce Degree from the University of Colombo and is a Chartered Management Accountant (U.K).

Indika Maligaspe

Indika has been with Rezgateway since its inception and is responsible for leading the engineering and technology innovations for the company’s products and services.

Indika was instrumental in kick starting open source, cloud, mobile and agile initiatives at Rezgateway and is currently working on building a new platform based on microservice architecture and cloud-to-device for Rezgateway’s product offerings.
